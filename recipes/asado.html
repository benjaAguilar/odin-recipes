Give a taste to Asado!

Making asado argentino

to create this amazing dish you gonna need a parrilla, a lot of wood logs and a good slice of meat. And be patient with the cook time...

  • Slice of meat
  • Wood logs
  • Parilla
  • salt

Steps

  1. Use the wood logs to create a fire inside the parrilla
  2. Put salt to the raw meat
  3. Once you have embers put the raw meat in the parrilla
  4. Wait until you see that the meat is bleeding on the upside part then turn the slice
  5. Wait for a few minutes more until you see that the other side is already cooked
  6. Enjoy your asado!

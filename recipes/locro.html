Making Locro argentino

to create this amazing dish you gonna need a pot, a lot of corns and a good differents types of meat. And be patient with the cook time...

  • Mix of meat slices
  • Pot
  • Corn
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put water on the pot and start cooking
  2. Put salt to the water
  3. Add the corns and wait until its cooked
  4. Put the slices of meat
  5. Wait for a few minutes more until you see that the meat is already cooked
  6. Enjoy your locro!

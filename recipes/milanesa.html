Making Milanesa argentina

to create this amazing dish you gonna need a pan, bread crumbs and a good type of meat. And be patient with the cook time...

  • Meat
  • Pan
  • Bread crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the meat in slices
  2. Drown with the eggs the raw meat and put salt
  3. Put the raw slices on the bread crumbs
  4. Put the slices on the pan
  5. Wait for a few minutes on both sides until you see that the meat is already cooked
  6. Enjoy your Milanesa!
